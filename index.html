⚽ How to Play

Goal: Move your chess pieces into the opponent's goal line (the gold line at the far end) to score!

  • Select a piece: Tap any white piece. It will glow gold.
  • Valid moves: Green circles show where you can move.
  • Move: Tap a green circle to move your piece there.
  • Capture: You can capture black pieces by moving onto their square.
  • Castling: Move your king two squares toward a rook to castle (if legal).
  • AI Turn: After your move, the AI (black) will automatically respond.
